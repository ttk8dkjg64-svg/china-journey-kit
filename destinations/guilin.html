Guilin & Yangshuo

Karst landscapes, Li River views, cycling villages, and a slower countryside rhythm.

Best way to experience it

Use Guilin as the transport base and Yangshuo as the atmosphere base. A Li River cruise or selected river segment, countryside cycling, and an evening show or food walk make a strong three-day plan.

Good for

Couples, photographers, families who want gentler days, and travelers who need a break from megacity pace.

Watch-outs

  • Rain and haze affect views; build flexibility into scenic days.
  • Some rural transfers are easier with hotel-arranged drivers.
  • Longji rice terraces need extra time and are seasonal.
