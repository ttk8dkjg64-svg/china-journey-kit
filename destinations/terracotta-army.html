Terracotta Army

Xi'an's essential archaeological site, best visited with a guide or strong audio context.

Visit strategy

Go early, start with the largest pit, and leave time for the museum context. A guide adds real value here because the scale and excavation history are easy to miss without explanation.

How to combine Xi'an

Plan one day for the Terracotta Army and one day for Xi'an city wall, Muslim Quarter food, Great Mosque area, and Tang-style night views.

Practical notes

  • Keep passport details consistent for tickets.
  • Traffic from central Xi'an can be slow.
  • Skip low-quality souvenir detours unless you actively want shopping.
