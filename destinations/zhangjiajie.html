Zhangjiajie

Sandstone pillars, misty viewpoints, cableways, glass bridges, and logistics that reward slower planning.

How many days

Spend at least two full days for Zhangjiajie National Forest Park. Add Tianmen Mountain or the Grand Canyon Glass Bridge if you have a third or fourth day.

Where to stay

Wulingyuan is convenient for the national park. Zhangjiajie city is better for Tianmen Mountain and rail/airport access. Choose based on the sight you will visit first in the morning.

Key advice

  • Weather changes quickly; keep one flexible day if photography matters.
  • Scenic buses, cable cars, and elevators mean travel time inside the park is real.
  • Book hotels that can help with park entrance names and local transfers.

Pair with Guilin for a nature-heavy route.
