Yunnan

Old towns, mountain air, minority cultures, lakes, and slow travel routes through Dali, Lijiang, and Shangri-La.

Route idea

Fly or rail into Kunming, continue to Dali for lake scenery, Lijiang for old-town energy, and Shangri-La if you can handle altitude and want Tibetan-influenced landscapes.

What to prepare

  • Layer clothing because altitude and evenings change the temperature.
  • Plan slower transfers than the map suggests.
  • Check hotel location carefully; old-town stays can be beautiful but hard with luggage.

Best for

Travelers who want China beyond the megacities and can spare at least five to seven days.
