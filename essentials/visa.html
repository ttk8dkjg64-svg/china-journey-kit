Visa and Transit

Entry rules change. Verify your nationality, route, ports, documents, and onward ticket before paying for flights.

240-hour transit basics

China's optimized visa-free transit policy allows eligible travelers from listed countries to transit through specified ports and stay in approved areas for up to 240 hours when traveling onward to a third country or region. This is not a universal visa waiver; route and eligibility matter.

Pre-booking checklist

  • Your passport nationality is eligible for the policy you intend to use.
  • Your entry and exit ports are covered.
  • Your itinerary continues to a third country or region if using transit.
  • Your flights, hotel bookings, and documents match your declared plan.

Official reference: China's visa-free transit policy announcement.

This page is planning guidance, not legal advice. Always verify with official immigration or consular channels before travel.

Good transit route

Seoul or Tokyo -> Shanghai -> Hong Kong can be valid in principle only if the current rules, ports, and nationality requirements match your case.
