Attraction Tickets

Many headline sights use timed reservations and passport-linked entry. Plan the ticket layer before trains and hotels lock you in.

General rules

  • Use the same passport spelling and number everywhere.
  • Book high-demand museums and scenic areas early.
  • Bring the physical passport to the gate.
  • Watch closure days, timed slots, and holiday crowds.

Where friction appears

Some official mini-programs are Chinese-first. If the booking flow blocks you, use a hotel concierge, reputable reseller, or guided product. Treat service fees as convenience costs, not as mandatory ticket prices.

High-demand examples

Forbidden City, popular Great Wall cable cars, Zhangjiajie scenic zones, Terracotta Army peak periods, and major museums can all sell out or become slow around holidays.
