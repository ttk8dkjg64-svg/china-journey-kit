Payments in China

Set up mobile payments before arrival and keep a backup for hotels, deposits, and edge cases.

What works

Travelers can usually use Alipay and WeChat Pay by linking international bank cards such as Visa or Mastercard. UnionPay, bank cards, and RMB cash can still be useful backups, especially for deposits or small edge cases.

Before you fly

  1. Download Alipay and WeChat.
  2. Register and verify identity where requested.
  3. Link at least one international card and test app access.
  4. Tell your bank you will travel in China.
  5. Carry some RMB cash for backup.

On the ground

Most merchants use QR payment. You either scan the merchant code or show your payment code. If a transaction fails, try the other app, a different card, or cash.

Official payment guidance says overseas visitors have several options, including mobile payment services, bank cards, and cash. Verify app rules before departure because onboarding can change.

Official reference: Payment service guide for overseas visitors to China.
