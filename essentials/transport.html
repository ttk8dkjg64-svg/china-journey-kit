Transport and Trains

Use high-speed rail between cities and metro or ride-hailing inside cities, with your passport details kept exact.

High-speed rail

China Railway's official English 12306 site and app support ticket purchase, changes, and refunds. Travelers use valid passports for real-name ticketing and station entry. Name and passport number must match exactly.

Booking steps

  1. Create a 12306 account or use a trusted reseller if you need simpler English support.
  2. Complete passport identity verification as early as possible.
  3. Book popular routes when sales open, especially near holidays.
  4. Arrive early at large stations; security and gates take time.

Official rail reference: 12306 China Railway English site and 12306 ticketing FAQ.

City transport

Metro systems in Beijing, Shanghai, Chengdu, Xi'an, and Guangzhou are efficient. Save hotel names in Chinese for taxis and ride-hailing.

Station rule

Carry the passport used to buy the ticket. A booking screenshot is not a replacement for identity checks.
