SIM, eSIM and Apps

Get online before you need a map, train code, payment QR, or translation help.

Best setup

Most travelers should prepare an eSIM or international roaming before landing, then decide whether they need a local SIM after arrival. A local SIM may require passport registration and can be useful for a Chinese phone number, but airport counters vary by city and arrival time.

Install before departure

  • Alipay and WeChat for payments, mini-programs, ride hailing, and messaging.
  • 12306 or official rail website access for train checks.
  • A translation app with offline language packs.
  • Maps that work for China and your hotel address saved in Chinese.

Practical safety

Keep your hotel address and emergency contact offline. Do not rely on SMS-only account recovery while switching SIMs.
