Hotels and Stays

Choose places that reduce friction: passport check-in support, good transit, and a realistic arrival plan.

Traveler-ready booking criteria

  • Can receive travelers with passport check-in needs and process passport registration.
  • Within walking distance of metro, rail station, or core attraction zone.
  • Clear English address and Chinese address saved offline.
  • 24-hour front desk for late international arrivals.

Where to stay

In Beijing, stay near Dongcheng, Wangfujing, or convenient metro lines. In Shanghai, consider People's Square, Jing'an, or the Bund area. For nature destinations, prioritize morning access over nightlife.

Deposits and payment

Some hotels may request deposits. Keep a mobile payment app, bank card, and small cash reserve ready.
